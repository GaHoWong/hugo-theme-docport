Create a `_footer.md` page in content root folder. Its content is what you get in the footer.

```bash
	content/
	├──	_footer.md
	└──	_footer.fr.md
```

{{%alert info%}}**Tip :** You can put a full HTML content in your .md file{{%/alert%}}
